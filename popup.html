StreetPeek

See the Street View near any rental listing before you book. The view shows the approximate area - exact addresses aren't revealed until after booking.

  1. Go to any listing page on a supported site
  2. Click the Street View button (bottom-right)
  3. Browse nearby amenities, public crime data in supported locations, and Street View

Independent tool. Not affiliated with or endorsed by Airbnb. All data is for informational purposes only and should not be used as the sole basis for booking decisions. Crime statistics, amenity counts, and location data may be incomplete, delayed, or approximate. Use your own judgment.

Works on Airbnb listing pages (.com, .co.uk, .ca, .com.au)
